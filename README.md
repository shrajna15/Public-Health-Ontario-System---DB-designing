## Public Health Ontario Database System 🏥

A relational DBMS designed to support evidence-based 
public health decision-making in Ontario.

### Tech Stack
- SQL — database implementation
- ERD Design — entity-relationship modeling
- DBMS concepts — normalization, access control, indexing

### System Coverage
- Patient registration & medical records
- Appointment scheduling & discharge summaries
- Prescription & medication management
- Billing & insurance provider coordination
- Surgery tracking & allergy records
- Laboratory & medical equipment management

### Design Highlights
- 13+ normalized entities with well-defined PKs and FKs
- Resolves real-world healthcare inefficiencies in billing,
  insurance coordination, and record management
- Built with scalability, data privacy, and disaster 
  recovery in mind
